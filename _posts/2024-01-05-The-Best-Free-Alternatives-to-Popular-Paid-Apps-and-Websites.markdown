### DaVinci Resolve, Shotcut, HitFilm Express
DaVinci Resolve is a powerful free video editing software with robust features for editing, color correction, audio post-production and visual effects. It includes everything from basic editing tools to advanced color grading controls. For simple yet capable free video editing, Shotcut is an excellent open-source option. It has a clean interface and supports a wide variety of formats. HitFilm Express provides advanced editing tools, visual effects, and compositing features for free.

### PowToon, VideoScribe, Explee 
PowToon is a favorite online tool for creating fun, engaging explainer animation videos and whiteboard style presentations. It has lots of templates and characters to make your presentations visually dynamic. VideoScribe takes a similar approach but focuses specifically on whiteboard animation. Both are great for infographics and processes. Explee allows anyone to create professional explainer videos through its library of ready-made templates and animation assets without design skills.

### Animaker, Moovly
Animaker is a breakthrough AI-powered platform offering studio quality animation and live-action videos. Its extensive media library and drag-and-drop editor make visual storytelling easy. Moovly takes a similar all-in-one approach but emphasizes marketing videos, presentations, online courses and other custom video content. Both are excellent for creating high-production videos without design or coding expertise. 

### KeePass, Secret Server
KeePass is a free, open-source password manager that encrypts login credentials in a secure database protected by a master password. It works across devices and includes basic features like password generation. Secret Server is a full-featured enterprise solution ideal for companies needing centralized, role-based access to secrets like API keys or cloud credentials.

### Aviary, Hipstamatic, Camera+
Aviary is a versatile free online photo editor for basic touch-ups and effects directly in the browser. Hipstamatic brings the fun of analog film photos to mobile with its retro filters and simulation of real camera toys. Camera+ is one of the most full-featured free camera and photo apps for iOS, covering everything from advanced controls to editing.

### Geotastic, Play Guessing Game
Geotastic recreates the fun of Geoguessr by dropping you into street views around the globe and challenging you to determine the location. Play Guessing Game takes a similar approach and is available on both web and mobile. Both are perfect for geography buffs and travelers to practice their global spatial skills.

### Pinterest, Flickr, Tumblr
Pinterest is best known as a visual discovery engine where users "pin" ideas, recipes and projects to virtual pinboards. Flickr remains popular among photographers for sharing and discovering creative commons licensed photos. Tumblr takes a microblogging approach focused on visual media like photos, GIFs and short-form videos across diverse topics.

### HashiCorp Vault, BeyondTrust PowerBroker  
HashiCorp Vault is an open-source secrets management tool providing encryption as a service capabilities. It securely stores, rotates and limits access to secrets like API keys, passwords and digital certificates. BeyondTrust PowerBroker is a full-fledged privileged access management solution ideal for highly regulated enterprises needing role-based access control of credentials across hybrid environments.

### NewTone in FL Studio, MIDI Controlled Auto-Tune VSTs
FL Studio's NewTone plugin provides powerful yet easy-to-use pitch correction and audio manipulation without spending on expensive Auto-Tune licenses. There are also free VST plugins like Waves Tune that enable precise auto-tuning via MIDI control directly in your DAW. Both are excellent for subtle or dramatic pitch correction in music production.

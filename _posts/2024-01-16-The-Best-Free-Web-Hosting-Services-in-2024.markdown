## Introduction
Web hosting has come a long way in recent years. Where once free hosting meant severely limited features and unreliable uptime, there are now many excellent free hosting options that give you everything you need to build a basic website. In this post, I'll review the top free web hosting providers and help you determine which one is the best fit for your needs.

### The Free Hosting Landscape Has Changed 
The free web hosting landscape has evolved significantly over the past decade. Companies now offer generous free hosting plans to attract new customers who may eventually upgrade to paid plans. While free plans have limitations compared to paid hosting, the features offered go well beyond what was previously available. Most free hosts now provide sufficient resources like storage, bandwidth, databases and basic email for simple personal websites and blogs.

### Factors to Consider
When choosing a free web host, it's important to evaluate factors like available resources, supported technologies, ease of use, reliability, and customer support. Consider your site's needs in terms of expected traffic and content volume. Also think about what tools and technologies you require, such as a content management system (CMS) like WordPress. Review independent hosting reviews to verify uptime and performance scores. Your choice will depend on balancing all these considerations within the feature constraints of free plans.

## 000Webhost
Owned by the popular web hosting company Hostinger, 000Webhost is one of the longest running free web hosting services. It offers 1 domain name, 300MB of storage space, and 1 MySQL database. The host uses the well-known cPanel control panel and also includes a free website builder and one-click apps for autoinstalling software like WordPress. However, limitations include a lack of email accounts, subdomains, and SSL certificates. Technical support is provided through community help forums rather than dedicated phone or ticket support. Still, for the basic features it supplies, 000Webhost remains a suitable free option for very simple personal sites.

## Infinity Free 
Infinity Free stands out from most other free web hosts by providing truly unlimited resources on its free hosting plan. This includes unlimited disk space, bandwidth, MySQL databases and more. The host also offers free domain privacy protection, automatic HTTPS/SSL encryption without additional fees, and a generous 45-day money back guarantee. In independent reviews, Infinity Free is frequently cited as one of the fastest free hosting services available. Its performance scores are on par with paid plans from other providers. The resource-rich free hosting makes Infinity Free well suited for more advanced personal or commercial websites.

## Dreamhost
DreamHost is widely known for its user-friendly hosting features and is a top choice for personal blogs, hobby sites and basic small business sites on a budget. The free DreamPress hosting plan provides 1GB of storage, unmetered bandwidth usage, PHP and MySQL support. Additional perks include a free domain name registration for one year and SSH access for command line use and administration. Webmail is also available directly through the free hosting control panel. WordPress, Joomla and other major CMS can be installed one-click through Fantastico. While the 1GB storage limit may constrain large sites, DreamHost remains highly capable for most simple free hosting needs. 

## Bluehost 
Bluehost has built a strong reputation for reliability and newbie-friendliness through its tutorial-rich knowledge base and user forums. The free hosting plan offers 50GB of storage space and unmetered bandwidth. It also uniquely includes a free domain registration valid for one year. Further features on the Bluehost free hosting include an SSL certificate for secure HTTPS encryption, malware scanning and automatic updates for security patches. The company's quality shared hosting infrastructure and focus on easy-to-use tools through cPanel make it suitable even for those unfamiliar with hosting management. Bluehost also stands out for its seamless WordPress setup process and migration tools. For simple personal sites, Bluehost delivers generous free hosting resources.

## Hostinger
Hostinger has earned a reputation as one of the most popular budget web hosts thanks to its affordable plans and strong performance metrics. The free Hostinger hosting plan provides 2GB of storage, unmetered bandwidth, and two email accounts - more resources than most competitors. Additional features include a website builder drag-and-drop interface, cPanel for advanced users, free SSL certificates, and one-click WordPress installation. While the free plan may not support the needs of high-traffic commercial sites indefinitely, it offers plenty for most light personal use cases. Hostinger's low pricing and massive scale also add to its appeal as a free web hosting provider.

## ByetHost
ByetHost has built a strong reputation in the hosting community through consistently reliable uptime and support. The free hosting plan provides 10GB of storage space, 250GB of bandwidth per month, and 3 MySQL databases - exceeding what most competitors offer. Additional features on ByetHost include Softaculous for one-click installation of over 300 scripts like WordPress and Joomla, cPanel for advanced users, and support for popular languages including PHP, Python and Ruby on Rails. ByetHost also stands out through its helpful knowledge base and 24/7 live chat support. For hobbyists and basic personal sites, ByetHost delivers generous free hosting resources.

## IPage
IPage has earned high marks across independent hosting reviews for performance, uptime and reliability. The free hosting plan provides 1GB of storage, shared yearly domain registration, and a shared MySQL database. Additional features include Softaculous one-click installers, cPanel for easy management, and similar tools found on other top free hosts. However, IPage stands alone in offering 24/7 phone and ticket support access to all users, whether on free or paid plans. This makes the provider highly approachable for new users needing assistance. While the resources are modest, IPage makes up for it through industry-leading support quality that few free competitors can match.

## Summary
All the top free web hosts covered deliver generous resources and capabilities for simple personal websites and blogs. When deciding, consider your needs, independent reviews of each provider, available support options and management tools. Free hosting has limitations that paid plans address like increased storage, dedicated resources and advanced features. But for starting a basic website on a budget, these providers represent some of the best free options for reliable, high-quality hosting without restricting features.
